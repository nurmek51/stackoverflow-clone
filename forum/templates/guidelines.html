Community Guidelines

Our Principles

DevOverflow is built on the following principles:

  • Be respectful and inclusive - Treat others with respect and kindness, regardless of their background or experience level.
  • Focus on quality - Strive to provide high-quality questions and answers that are clear, concise, and helpful.
  • Share knowledge freely - Contribute your expertise to help others learn and grow.
  • Give credit where it's due - Acknowledge the work of others when you build upon it.

Expected Behavior

As a member of the DevOverflow community, you are expected to:

  • Be respectful and considerate in your interactions with others
  • Use appropriate language and avoid offensive content
  • Provide constructive feedback and criticism
  • Respect the privacy and personal boundaries of others
  • Follow the site's rules and guidelines

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable on DevOverflow:

  • Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
  • Posting offensive, inappropriate, or explicit content
  • Spamming or posting promotional content
  • Plagiarism or copyright infringement
  • Creating multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions
  • Deliberately posting misleading or incorrect information

Content Guidelines

Questions

  • Search before asking to avoid duplicate questions
  • Be specific and provide all relevant details
  • Use clear, descriptive titles
  • Format your code properly for readability
  • Add appropriate tags to categorize your question

Answers

  • Answer the question directly and completely
  • Provide explanations, not just code
  • Cite sources and references when appropriate
  • Format your answer for readability

Comments

  • Use comments to request clarification or suggest improvements
  • Keep comments concise and on-topic
  • Avoid extended discussions in comments

Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account termination

The severity of the action will depend on the nature and frequency of the violation.

Reporting Violations

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it by:

  • Using the "Flag" option on the post
  • Contacting a moderator
  • Emailing us at moderation@devoverflow.example.com
